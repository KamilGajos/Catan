About "Catan – Minecraft World"
🎮 A pure HTML/CSS/JS clone of the classic board game.
🏗️ Procedural hexagonal map with unique Minecraft-style textures.
⚙️ Full resource management (wood, wool, wheat, clay, stone).
🏪 Trading system with bank and special port ratios.
🃏 Development cards: Knight, Monopoly, Road Building, Victory Points.
☠️ Robber mechanic triggered by rolling a 7.
🛣️ Longest road & largest army bonuses.
🧠 Built as a single-page interactive game – no frameworks, no backend.
✨ Clean UI with toast notifications and floating animations.

How to Play
🏆 First to 20 VP wins.
🔄 Setup: each player places 1 settlement + 1 road forward, then again in reverse order.
🎲 Roll dice: produces resources on tiles matching the sum (except 7).
🏗️ Build: roads (wood + clay), settlements (wood, wool, wheat, clay), cities (3 stone + 2 wheat).
🏪 Trade: 4:1 with bank, or better ratios at ports (3:1, 2:1 for specific resources).
🃏 Cards: Knight (move robber & steal), Monopoly (take one resource from all), Road Building (2 free roads), Victory Point (hidden).
☠️ Robber: on a 7, discard half if >8 cards, then move robber to block a tile and steal from a player.
🛣️ Longest road (≥5 segments, unbroken) gives +2 VP.
⚔️ Largest army (≥3 knight cards) gives +2 VP.
📦 You may play 1 development card per turn (not the same turn you bought it).
